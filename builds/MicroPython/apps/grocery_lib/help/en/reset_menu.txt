RESET EVERYTHING
Delete all your data and start over.

OPTIONS:
- Clear All Lists: Deletes all your lists.
- Clear Pantry: Deletes everything At Home.

WARNING:
This deletes things forever.
We will ask "Are you sure?" first.

SHORTCUTS:
Arrows: Move around.
Center: Choose an option.
BACK: Go back.
F3: Show/Hide this help.

PURPOSE:
The Reset menu provides a way to wipe specific parts of your data when they are no longer needed. Whether you are starting a fresh year of shopping or simply want to reorganize your entire home inventory from scratch, these options allow for a clean break while providing safety prompts to prevent accidental data loss.
