LIST SETUP
Change the name or budget for this list.

OPTIONS:
- Rename: Change the list name.
- Budget: Set a spending limit (0 = none).
- Delete: Remove this list forever.

SHORTCUTS:
Arrows: Move around.
Center: Change or Confirm.
BACK: Go back.
F3: Show/Hide this help.

PURPOSE:
List Setup provides the necessary tools to customize your shopping experience for specific needs. Whether you are renaming a list for a special event or setting a strict budget limit to control your spending, these options give you full control over your shopping data, helping you stay organized and within your financial goals.
