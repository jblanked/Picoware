MY LISTS
Pick a list or make a new one.

HOW IT WORKS:
- See all your lists.
- Shows how many items you got so far.
- Shows budget if you set one.

SHORTCUTS:
Any Key: Start searching.
Up/Down: Move around.
Center: Open list.
Right: Open List Setup.
Home: Go back to Main Menu.
F3: Show/Hide this help.
F4: Jump to Shopping List.

PURPOSE:
This view allows you to manage multiple shopping lists for different stores or occasions. You can see your progress at a glance, including the number of items collected and your remaining budget for each run, making it easy to track your spending across different shopping trips.

SEARCHING:
You can filter your list collection instantly by typing on the keyboard. This is extremely helpful when you have many lists and need to find a specific one quickly without having to scroll through the entire view. The results update in real-time as you type, providing immediate visual feedback.
